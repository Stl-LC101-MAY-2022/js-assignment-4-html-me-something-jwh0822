NFL Stadiums Visited

In 2016, myself and 3 brothers and 5 nephews started to visit different NFL stadiums. The journey started with us visiting Heinz field in Pittsburg, PA. The following year we caught a game at AT&T stadium in Dallas, TX. From their we went to the legendary Lambeau field in Green Bay, Wisconsin.

On our list of stadiums to visit in the comings years is the Vet in Philadelphia, PA., Arrowhead in Kansas City, MO., and the Superdome in New Orleans, LA.
